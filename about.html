About Tsitsalagi

How this community board works.

Tsitsalagi is an independent, nonpartisan, citizen-led public board. It is built to make local listings, useful resources, and community issues easier to find without requiring an account.

Review first

Submissions are reviewed

Listings, issues, and resource suggestions are reviewed before appearing publicly. Approval means the post was accepted for publication; it does not mean Tsitsalagi independently verified every claim.

Privacy

Keep private documents off the site

Do not submit CDIB cards, citizenship cards, driver licenses, birthdates, Social Security numbers, payment details, passwords, medical records, or anything you do not want public.

Safety

Verify before acting

Listings are public community posts, not endorsements. Meet safely, verify details, and use official websites for programs, deadlines, laws, services, benefits, and final answers.

What belongs here

Useful, public-safe information

Tsitsalagi works best when posts are calm, specific, easy to understand, and safe to publish.

Listings

  • Items, services, jobs, events, rides, free items, lost-and-found notices, and community help
  • Clear title, area, category, price or cost, description, and public contact method
  • Photos are allowed when they are safe to publish

Issues

  • Local concerns, hazards, public-service questions, and community problems
  • Specific location, facts, status, and source links when possible
  • One clear public question or request for accountability

Not allowed

  • Illegal items, weapons, drugs, alcohol, tobacco, adult content, scams, fake documents, or stolen goods
  • Private documents, personal attacks, threats, harassment, or sensitive information about others
  • Payment details, passwords, ID numbers, or private records

Safety

Use the board safely

Tsitsalagi.com Community Board is a public information board. It is not a payment service, buyer-protection service, or government website.

Protect privacy

Do not post private documents, numbers, birthdates, signatures, passwords, medical records, legal records, or payment details.

Do not post prohibited items

No weapons, ammunition, alcohol, tobacco, nicotine, cannabis, drugs, adult content, scams, stolen items, fake documents, or illegal materials.

Verify before acting

Use official sources for deadlines, benefits, forms, laws, elections, and public services. Meet safely and verify posts yourself.

Tsitsalagi.com Community Board is an independent, nonpartisan, citizen-led community board.

Browse local listings, useful resources, and documented public issues in one place. Use official sources for voter registration, benefits, services, laws, and deadlines.

Listings are community posts, not endorsements. Always verify information for yourself and meet safely.

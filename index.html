Subject: Request for Public Answer on EMS Coverage

What specific action do you support to improve EMS response, ambulance coverage, and emergency medical care inside the Cherokee Nation Reservation without simply relying on county governments?

Please explain whether you support Cherokee Nation building its own stronger EMS system, funding counties, or another model.

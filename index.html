Records

Build a public memory bank

A public records log prevents the same questions from being asked over and over with no shared answer.

Suggested log columns

Date sent • Office • Topic • Request text • Response due • Response received • Documents received • Follow-up needed

Privacy rule

Redact private addresses, phone numbers, medical facts, minors’ names, signatures, IDs, and citizenship numbers before posting anything public.

Document rule

Post the original document, a short neutral summary, and a link to the next action. Do not change quoted answers.

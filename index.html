Rules & safety

Keep it useful, safe, and low-liability

This should start as a moderated listing board, not a full marketplace. Do not handle payments. Do not promise buyer protection. Do not collect private IDs or citizenship documents.

Do not collect

  • CDIB numbers or citizenship numbers
  • birth dates, IDs, signatures, or voter forms
  • private medical, financial, or legal records
  • passwords, payments, or card information

Do not allow

  • firearms, ammunition, weapons, or explosives
  • alcohol, tobacco, nicotine, cannabis, THC, CBD, or drugs
  • adult content, counterfeit goods, stolen items, or fake documents
  • hazardous materials or anything illegal

Good listing rules

  • clear title, area, category, price, and contact method
  • 30-day expiration unless renewed
  • one listing per item/service
  • moderator can remove anything for safety or credibility

Tsitsalagi is an independent, nonpartisan, citizen-led community board and issue hub.

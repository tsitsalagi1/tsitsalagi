How to use Tsitsalagi.com Community Board

Browse, submit, verify

  1. Browse listings, issues, and resources without creating an account.

  3. Submissions are reviewed before they appear publicly.
  4. Contact posters directly and verify everything yourself.

Privacy first

Do not send private documents

Tsitsalagi.com Community Board does not need CDIB cards, citizenship cards, birthdates, Social Security numbers, passwords, payment information, or private records. Do not submit anything you do not want public.

Rules & safety

Use the board safely

This is a public community board, not a payment service or buyer-protection service. Protect your information, use public meeting places, verify posts yourself, and use official sources for programs, elections, services, and deadlines.

Protect your privacy

  • Do not post CDIB numbers or citizenship numbers
  • Do not post birth dates, IDs, signatures, or voter forms
  • Do not share private medical, financial, or legal records
  • Do not send passwords, payment information, or card numbers

Do not allow

  • firearms, ammunition, weapons, or explosives
  • alcohol, tobacco, nicotine, cannabis, THC, CBD, or drugs
  • adult content, counterfeit goods, stolen items, or fake documents
  • hazardous materials or anything illegal

Make a clear post

  • Use a clear title, town or area, category, price, and public contact method
  • Say whether the item is free, for sale, trade, or contact-for-price
  • Use facts and source links when submitting a public issue
  • Ask for a correction or removal if something is wrong

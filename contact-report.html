CONTACT / REPORT

Contact Tsitsalagi.com Community Board.

Use this page for corrections, takedown requests, unsafe listings, broken links, public issue corrections, or general messages. It works on phones and computers because it gives you more than one way to contact Tsitsalagi.com Community Board.

Send a message

Type your message below, then open Gmail, copy the message, or try your device’s default email app.

This page does not send the email by itself. It opens Gmail or your email app with the address, subject, and message filled in so you can review and send it.

What to include

For a correction or report, include the listing or issue title, what is wrong, and what should be changed or removed. You do not need to include a website link.

Do not send private documents, CDIB cards, driver licenses, passwords, payment details, or sensitive personal information.

Tsitsalagi.com Community Board is an independent, nonpartisan, citizen-led community board.

After sending, you can return to Tsitsalagi.com Community Board using the Home button above.

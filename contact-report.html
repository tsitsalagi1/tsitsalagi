What to include

For a correction or report, include the listing or issue title, link, what is wrong, and what should be changed or removed.

Do not send private documents, CDIB cards, driver licenses, passwords, payment details, or sensitive personal information.

Tsitsalagi is an independent, nonpartisan, citizen-led community board.

After sending, you can return to Tsitsalagi using the Home button above.

CONTACT / REPORT

Contact Tsitsalagi.

Use this page for corrections, takedown requests, unsafe listings, broken links, public issue corrections, or general messages. This works on phones and computers because it offers more than one way to send the message.

Send a message

Type your message below, then open Gmail, copy the message, or try your device’s default email app.

This page does not send the email by itself. It opens Gmail or your email app with the address, subject, and message filled in so you can review and send it.

What to include

For a correction or report, include the listing or issue title, link, what is wrong, and what should be changed or removed.

Do not send private documents, CDIB cards, driver licenses, passwords, payment details, or sensitive personal information.

Tsitsalagi is an independent, nonpartisan, citizen-led community board.

After sending, you can return to Tsitsalagi using the Home button above.

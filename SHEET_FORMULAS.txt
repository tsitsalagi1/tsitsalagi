TSITSALAGI PHOTO APPROVAL FORMULAS

After you install the Apps Script and Worker, update your public approved tabs so photos can flow to the website.

1) Submit a Listings tab columns should be:
A Timestamp
B Listing title
C Listing category
D Area / town / county
E Price / cost
F Listing description
G Public contact method
H Tags / keywords
I Photo URL
J Approved

2) Submit a Issue tab columns should be:
A Timestamp
B Issue title
C Issue category
D Area / town / county
E Severity / urgency
F Issue description
G Public contact method
H Tags / keywords
I Photo URL
J Approved

3) Approved Listings row 1 headers should be:
Approved | Title | Category | Area | Price | Description | Contact | Posted | Expires | Tags | Photo

Paste this into Approved Listings cell A2:

=IFERROR(FILTER({'Submit a Listings'!J2:J,'Submit a Listings'!B2:B,'Submit a Listings'!C2:C,'Submit a Listings'!D2:D,'Submit a Listings'!E2:E,'Submit a Listings'!F2:F,'Submit a Listings'!G2:G,IF('Submit a Listings'!A2:A="",,TO_DATE('Submit a Listings'!A2:A)),IF('Submit a Listings'!A2:A="",,TO_DATE('Submit a Listings'!A2:A+30)),'Submit a Listings'!H2:H,'Submit a Listings'!I2:I},REGEXMATCH(LOWER(TRIM('Submit a Listings'!J2:J)),"^(yes|approved|true|y|1)$")),"")

4) Approved Issues row 1 headers should be:
Approved | Title | Category | Status | Area | Question | Ask | Source | LastUpdated | Photo

Paste this into Approved Issues cell A2:

=IFERROR(FILTER({'Submit a Issue'!J2:J,'Submit a Issue'!B2:B,'Submit a Issue'!C2:C,'Submit a Issue'!E2:E,'Submit a Issue'!D2:D,'Submit a Issue'!F2:F,IF('Submit a Issue'!B2:B="",,"Review and help identify the correct office, department, or community contact. Public contact: "&'Submit a Issue'!G2:G),IF('Submit a Issue'!B2:B="",,""),IF('Submit a Issue'!A2:A="",,TO_DATE('Submit a Issue'!A2:A)),'Submit a Issue'!I2:I},REGEXMATCH(LOWER(TRIM('Submit a Issue'!J2:J)),"^(yes|approved|true|y|1)$")),"")

Workflow after setup:
- User submits listing or issue with optional photo.
- Cloudflare Worker uploads photo to R2.
- Worker posts the form fields and photo URL into Submit a Listings or Submit a Issue.
- You review the row.
- Change Approved to yes.
- It automatically appears on Tsitsalagi with the photo.
